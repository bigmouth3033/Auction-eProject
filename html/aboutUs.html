About Us






Who We Are?

We are AuctionsTable an organization that sells valuable items and goods through auctions. We have a passion for connecting buyers and sellers of rare and exquisite products.



What We Do?

We provide all services for auctions with more than 700 expert auction houses worldwide, adding millions of unique items each year. Whether you are looking for a vintage Car, a Picasso painting, or a Ming dynasty vase, we have something for everyone.



How We Started?

We started as a small family business in 1998, organizing local auctions in our hometown. Over the years, we expanded our network and expertise, and launched our online platform in 2010. Since then, we have grown to become one of the leading auction corporation in the world.



Why We Do It?

We believe that auctions are more than just transactions. They are stories, histories, and experiences. They are opportunities to discover new treasures and share them with others. They are ways to celebrate the beauty and diversity of human creativity and craftsmanship.

Our Values

Trust

We build trust with our customers, partners, and employees by being honest, transparent, and reliable.

We deliver quality in everything we do, from our products and services to our customer support and communication.
